How to Play - Keyboard

Walk Left Arrow & Right Arrow
Jump Spacebar Keyboard
Throw Bottle 'D' Letter Keyboard

Game Info

1. Collect bottles to throw at the chickens.
2. Collect coins to restore the health bar to 100%.
3. There are two types of chickens (normal, small). Both types can be defeated with a single bottle.
4. At the end of the game, the Endboss appears with its own health bar and can be defeated after 4 bottle hits.
Flip your device to join the adventure!
